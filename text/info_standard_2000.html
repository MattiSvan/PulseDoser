Funnel: Standard 2000 g/s

Standard setup (High capacity)

  • Speed when pulsing: 100 %
  • Pulse ON time: 2 s
  • Calibration value: ~2000 g/s

Gives large pulses (2000 g x 2s = 4000g), ideal for high-capacity demands (40 kg/min+).

Mid setup (Versatile use)

  • Speed when pulsing: 50-60 %
  • Pulse ON time: 1-1.5 s
  • Calibration value: ~1000-1200 g/s

Suitable for small to mid-size fish or larger fish if high capacity isn't required. Can feed up to ~30-40 kg/min while maintaining small doses.

Smolt setup (Small doses)

  • Speed when pulsing: 30 %
  • Pulse ON time: 1 s
  • Calibration value: ~600 g/s

Allows smaller doses for smaller fish, but has low max capacity
